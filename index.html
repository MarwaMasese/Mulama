Hello There

We Are ZionLab

The goal of ZionLab valley innovation lab is to bring a greater change in Africa, by investing in our continent’s aspiring youth entrepreneurs-to-be. We’re enabling them to build sustainable innovations, which tackle their country’s biggest challenges with technology Inspired by WordTech, it will be run in different Churches every year in cohorts. WORDTECH is a brainstorming technique that inspires the youth to come up with purpose driven innovative ideas from the Bible. Our target is to successfully kick-start innovations aligned with our selection criteria across the continent. We are doing this by providing the capital, resources and guidance necessary to begin from scratch.. With both a physical and virtual lab ,ZionLab Valley Innovation Lab targets to raise a 100 WORDTECH inspired innovations and sustainable business that are Youth led. This will be able to create over 1000 new direct job opportunities through the Youth and the newly established companies.

OUR INITIATIVES

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

1.WordTech

ZionLab Valley Innovation Lab introduces a new first of a kind Innovation concept called WORDTECH. WORDTECH is the world's first brainstorming technique that takes the culture, the principles and the ideologies of the Word of God/the Bible and uses them to transform society through innovation. Through the WORDTECH Concept, we support young Africans who are passionate about building local solutions to their most pressing community problems. We believe that the greatest amount of impact can be achieved through WORDTECH inspired innovations, and ZionLab Valley Innovation Lab is committed to birthing these across the continent with technology.

2.Capacity

Innovative thinking is necessary to spar creation and multiplication of products and services that are truly problem based and have the potential to impact society.ZionLab Valley Innovation Lab provides sufficient capacity to help the Youth build sustainable innovations through WordTechand,We will be able to involve corporates and any interested partners in building capacity for Innovation.

3.Anagkazo-Coming soon

The NorthEight Valley Innovation Lab necessitates innovation through crowdfunding,the crowdfunding platform is under development and will enable Youth to raise funding both locally and Internationally to help fund the development of their innovations.This is through the Anagkazo,a compelling principle.

4.Word Exploits-John 1:1-4 KJV-Coming soon

Word exploits are weekly meetup that enables the Youth come and share any ideas that they have identified directly from the bible to help them come up with a wordtech idea..Anyone interested in innovating using the wordtech brainstorming concept is invited to this events

5.Ingenuity Innovation Training(IIT)

The NorthEight valley Innovation Lab is working in an innovation development curriculum.the Curriculum will equip youth innovators with quality knowledge and skills that will empower them to develop sustainable businesses from the innovative ideas that they come up with

6.Current WordTech Startups

ZionLab Valley Innovation lab boast of innovations that are under development of which two have already USD40000 for development and commercialisation,this includes Kitabu Africa
